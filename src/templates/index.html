NEURAL PIPELINE

Inference & Prediction Architecture

Our prediction engine utilizes a fine-tuned EfficientNet-B0 running natively in PyTorch. Raw images are ingested, dynamically resized to 224x224, and normalized using strict ImageNet variance tensors. The data then cascades through 5.3 million parameters, ending in a Softmax classification layer that generates an absolute probability vector across all 10 target vehicle models.

OPTIMIZATION

Extreme Regularization

To combat neural overfitting, the training pipeline actively mutates the dataset using massive RandomErasing, Affine rotations, and dynamic ColorJittering. The model was stabilized using AMP (Automatic Mixed Precision) and Cosine Annealing Learning Rates.

EXPLAINABILITY

GradCAM Spatial Interceptor

This is not a black-box model. During inference, we attach a `full_backward_hook` to the final convolutional layer. By multiplying the forward feature maps against the backward gradients, the engine calculates a spatial heatmap of exactly what pixels influenced the AI's final decision. Our custom 3x3 grid-analyzer then automatically calculates the bounding boxes of the highest activating features (e.g. Grille, Headlight, or Roof).
